Data Deletion Request for Junkyard Business

At Diaconu C Nicolae PFA, we respect your privacy and provide you with the option to request the deletion of your personal data collected through our game Junkyard Business.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to xtrweb@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".
  2. In the email, include your installation ID/user ID and a description of the data you wish to be deleted.
  3. We will process your request and delete your data from our systems within the required timeframe, typically within 30 days.

Data stored by Google Play Services

This game uses Google Play Games services for authentication and game progress storage. Users can remove the game from their Google Play Games account to delete associated Google Play data by:

  1. Opening Google Play Games app
  2. Going to Settings
  3. Selecting "Delete Play Games account & data"
  4. Choosing Junkyard Business

Types of Data That Can Be Deleted

The types of data that can be deleted include:

  • App activity data
  • Device or other IDs
  • Payment information - payment data is processed by Google Play and is not stored on our servers

Data Retention Periods

Some data may be retained for a limited period as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

How to Find Your Installation ID

To request the deletion of your data, we need your Firebase Installation ID. You can find your Installation ID within the game. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the Junkyard Business game.
  2. Go to the Settings.
  3. Find the section labeled Firebase Installation ID.
  4. Your Installation ID will be displayed as: Firebase Installation ID: [ID]
  5. Include this ID in your data deletion request email.
